Help save the word by participating in a one hour online software usability study. Our goal is to help people effectively discuss contentious issues using open source software.

To participate you will need to:

  • Have a computer that supports video conferencing and has a camera
  • Allow us to record the session
  • Sign a non disclosure agreement (NDA)
  • Speak english fluently

If you meet all these requirements please choose a time below and fill out the 10 questions that follow.

We have a limited number of spots. Filling out the form does not guarantee participation in the study.
