Before I Build Your App

I enjoy hearing everyones app ideas and pointing them in the right direction. Before I invest significant time on an idea these are the steps that should be done to reduce the risk of the idea.

  • Competition Research - Feature Comparison
  • Who is your Customer?
  • Market Research
  • Pitch scripts

Competition Research - Feature Comparison

...there is nothing new under the sun.
— Ecclesiastes 1:9, The Bible
It is rare that a completely fresh idea comes around. Sure, no one is doing what you are considering, but what is close. What are people doing today, wvwn without technology.

Be ready to provide

  • What are people doing today without technology?
  • Create a feature comparison chart .
  • What is the compelling reason to switch to your solution?
